During my first semester we had to create a physical board game. This involved designing the games mechanics and also creating the components for the game.
When making the board game I gained an insight into how games can be design to be fun and balanced.

My board game was a roguelike dungeon crawler for 2-4 players. The games difficulty would increase as players progressed through the floors of the dungeon.
